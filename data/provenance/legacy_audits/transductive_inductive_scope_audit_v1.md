# Transductive / inductive scope audit v1

**Preferred canonical wording:** `transductive non-herb representation with inductive unseen-herb encoding`.

The D12 manuscript is consistent with this boundary. Held-out herbs are absent from training and are encoded at query time from label-free herb–compound context. Compounds and all 2,309 targets are shared transductive nodes with trainable embeddings.

No D12 claim of fully inductive graph learning, unseen-target prediction, de novo target discovery, target-inductive evaluation, or all-nodes-unseen evaluation was found. Earlier overclaims are documented as resolved. Future edits and figures should use phrase guards because shortening the setting to “inductive model” would be ambiguous.
