# Restricted Materials Exclusion Audit

Status: PASS

The public package excludes raw held-out labels, raw test positive pairs, target-attributable held-out records, score matrices, prediction matrices, model checkpoints, controller-held materials, restricted third-party raw source records, and third-party raw records with unclear redistribution rights.

The included files are public-facing documentation, final figures, source notes or aggregate source data, tables, aggregate metrics, protocol summaries, audit summaries, licence files, manifest, and checksums.
